Problem Most people use government national forest/national park websites to book
their camping trips, which are typically not very user friendly and complicated
to use. The Happy Camper app makes it simple and easy to book camping
trips at your favorite camping locations. After conducting multiple user interviews and usability tests, I was able to
successfully design an app that met the user's needs and solve their problems
when planning camping trips. Solution The Happy Camper App A travel app making it simple to find and
book popular campgrounds across the nation. My Design Process Tools Miro
